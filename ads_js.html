What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment. These materials can affect air, water, and land, causing damage to humans, animals, and plants.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution: Smoke from vehicles and factories.
  • Water Pollution: Waste dumped into rivers and oceans.
  • Land Pollution: Plastic waste and chemicals in soil.
  • Noise Pollution: Loud sounds from traffic and machines.

How to Save the Environment

  • Plant more trees
  • Avoid plastic bags
  • Use public transport
  • Save water and electricity
  • Keep your surroundings clean
